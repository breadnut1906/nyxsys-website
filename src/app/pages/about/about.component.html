How We Began

Founded in June 2014, Nyxsys Philippines, Inc. has quickly become a pioneer in Digital Media and Business Technology Solutions. What started as a bold spin-off from an Out-Of-Home (OOH) advertising company dating back to the 1990s has evolved into a powerhouse for digital transformation.

At Nyxsys, we’re not just about solutions; we’re about experiences. With a dynamic team of highly-trained professionals, we push the boundaries of innovation in Corporate Business Solutions, Digital Signage, and Out-Of-Home Advertising. Our digital-first approach ensures that our services are at the forefront of technology, offering end-to-end, cutting-edge solutions designed to redefine your business potential.
